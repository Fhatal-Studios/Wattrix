    Privacy & Cookies — FAQ

    What cookies and storage do you use?

    Sivuston toiminnan ja turvallisuuden kannalta välttämättömät evästeet (strictly necessary) are always on. Optional cookies include Analytics and Ads/Marketing. We also use localStorage to keep your calculator inputs on your device.

    Legal bases (EU GDPR / ePrivacy)
    • Strictly necessary: allowed without consent to provide a service you request (Art. 6(1)(b)/(f)).
    • Analytics and Ads/Marketing: require prior, freely given, specific, informed, unambiguous opt-in consent (Art. 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw anytime.
    Do you share data with third parties?

    Only if you opt in. Analytics data may be sent to Google Analytics 4. Ads data may be shared with Google AdSense and ad partners.

    Retention
    • Consent record: stored in your browser until changed or cleared.
    • Analytics/ads cookies: per vendor defaults; see Google’s policies.
    • Calculator inputs: remain in your browser until you reset or clear site data.
    How do I change my choice or exercise rights?
    • Click to reopen preferences.
    • Clear site data in your browser to remove local data.
    • For GDPR rights (access, erasure, etc.), contact: privacy@fhatal.com.
