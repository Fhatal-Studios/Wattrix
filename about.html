About Wattrix

The privacy-focused, fast, and free EV trip planner.

Why Wattrix?

Planning an EV trip shouldn't be complicated. Most planners require accounts, sync data to the cloud, or are overloaded with ads. Wattrix is different:

  • Privacy First: No accounts. No tracking pixels for the calculator. All math happens in your browser.
  • Instant: Change a value, see the result. No loading spinners or server round-trips.
  • Universal: Works for Tesla, Hyundai, Volkswagen, Rivian—any EV where you know the battery size and efficiency.

Privacy & FAQ

What cookies do you use?

Strictly necessary cookies (for security/load balancing) are always on. We optionally use Google Analytics to understand site usage, but only if you click "Accept" on the banner. You can manage this anytime.

Where is my data stored?

Your calculator inputs (battery size, trip distance, etc.) are stored in your browser's localStorage. This means if you come back to the site on the same device, your settings are remembered. We do not send this data to any server.

Can I use this offline?

Because the logic runs in the browser, once the page is loaded, you can actually go offline and continue calculating! The app is designed to be lightweight and resilient.
